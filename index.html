About

Hi, my name is Le. Get your favorite drink and let's me tell you a story. In 2013, I was a freshman who wanted to become the greatest illustrator ever. As I navigated myself through college, my dream was crushed when I realized that (1) my passion was slowly leaving me as things got harder, and (2) I was doubting myself that I wasn't good enough. So I packed my bag and went to study Engineering. During my time in Engineering school, I was busy catching up homeworks after homeworks that I forgot how to draw and appreciate beauty in art. Until now when I started to learn React/web dev as a way to gain solid skills, I slowly felt a glimpse of my old passion coming back to me. Is it the UI design? Is it the eye-pleasing colors? I can't tell, but I have found meanings in creating these online widgets that someday help my users achieve their goals, and love looking at.
